  • The Constitution of The Saturated Unicorn
  • 1 Power of Authority
    • 1.1 The Commissioner
      • 1.1.1 The Saturated Unicorn (TSU) will have one league commissioner who, once appointed,will remain in office until: 1. Death 2. Retirement 3. Impeachment or 4.Imprisonment.
      • 1.1.2 Current TSU Commissioner - Noah Newport
      • 1.1.3 Any issue, dispute, or grievance not explicitly stated herein to be voted on by the Senior Council will solely lie within the jurisdiction of the Commissioner. All decisions made by the commissioner are final.
      • 1.1.4 Any dispute or decision directly involving the commissioner will be dealt with by a majority vote of the Senior Council.
      • 1.1.5 Any non-unanimous vote of the Senior Council must be approved by the Commissioner.
      • 1.1.6 The commissioner holds an unquestioned power to overrule or rule on any decision or issue he/she deems necessary for intervention or final decision.
      • 1.1.7 Any issue between the commissioner and Lord of the Council needing approval or ruling will be handled via the following process
    • 1.2 The Senior Council
      • 1.2.1 The Senior Council will consist of three senior members of the league, excluding the commissioner and will be led by the Lord of the Council (LOC).
      • 1.2.2 The alternate Senior Council member shall not have any special powers under normal league conditions. The alternate is to only be used when the commissioner and/or a Senior Council member(s) are involved in an issue that requires a deciding vote of the Senior Council or a member of the Senior Council cannot adequately fulfill their role for a period of time.
      • 1.2.3 Senior Council membership is a lifetime appointment (lifetime of active participation in the league). The same parameters for vacating office, outlined for the Commissioner in Article I Section 1, will apply to Senior Council members.
      • 1.2.4 If a vacancy occurs in the Senior Council the remaining Senior Council members and the Commissioner will take a vote to nominate a new member.
      • 1.2.5 All disputes, alterations, malfunctions, or questions stated to be within the jurisdiction of the Senior Council herein this document will be voted on and approved with by a majority vote of the Senior Council.
      • 1.2.6 Any time “proven” is stated within this document, this can be understood as meaning solid proof was supplied to and reviewed by the entire Senior Council and a majority vote ruled in favor of the issue supported, by said evidence.
      • 1.2.7 If a conflict of interest exists for a member or members of The Senior Council on a issue necessary for a Senior Council vote or ruling, the following procedure will be followed to replace the member(s) until the issue is resolved
    • 1.3 Lord of the Council

      • 1.3.2 Issues that are determined to be a conflict of interest for the Commissioner will be handled by the Lord of the Council with equal powers to that of the Commissioner.
  • 2 Owners Code of Conduct
    • 2.1 Conduct
      • 2.1.1 Franchise owners must act with the best interest of their team in mind at ALL times!
      • 2.1.2 Collusion will NOT be tolerated
      • 2.1.3 Cheating of all types will not be tolerated
      • 2.1.4 At minimum, owners who “quit” on their team at any point during the season (including the playoffs) will face a mandatory 1 year ban (minimum) from the league.
  • 3 Membership & Payout
    • 3.1 Teams & Platform
      • 3.1.1 TSU consists of 12 teams, no more no less
      • 3.1.2 A fan of Alabama, Florida, or Ohio State shall never be permitted entry into TSU.
      • 3.1.3 TSU will forever be an ESPN league unless a majority league vote decides to move to another host.
    • 3.2 Dues & Entry Fees
      • 3.2.1 The cost of entry for the 2019 season will be $140
      • 3.2.2 All monies must be paid before the draft order is set
      • 3.2.3 All monies will be paid to the commissioner and he/she will be responsible for holding the money until the conclusion of the season.
      • 3.2.4 In season, all monies shall be kept in the official TSU bank account
      • 3.2.5 Weekly payouts will be made at the conclusion of the season.
    • 3.3 Payout
      • 3.3.1 The total prize money for the 2019 season will be $1680 and will be distributed as follows
    • 3.4 Trophy
      • 3.4.1 The name of the league champion will be engraved onto the trophy at the conclusion of each season.
      • 3.4.2 If the league champion lives outside of Knoxville it will be their sole responsibility to arrange for trophy pickup.
      • 3.4.3 In the event the league champion is unable to make an appearance the trophy must be present at the following year’s draft!
      • 3.4.4 The last place finisher of the previous season MUST be present at the following year’s draft (if they are still a member of TSU)
    • 3.5 The Randy
      • 3.5.1 The Randy will be awarded to the team finishing in 12th (last) place of the playoff structure.
      • 3.5.2 Once contracted, The Randy must be openly displayed in the recipient’s home. Failure to do so will result in penalty determined by the league commissioner.
      • 3.5.3 The Randy must also be worn for the entirety of the following season’s draft.
      • 3.5.4 The recipient must print their name, team name, and season contracted on The Randy.
      • 3.5.5 Failure to adorn The Randy at the following years draft will lead to an automatic expulsion from the league.
      • 3.5.6 The only way a person infected with The Randy can remain an active member of TSU and not adorn The Randy at the following years draft, is for a fellow member to volunteer as a sacrifice in the infected persons place.
  • 4 Team Roster & Lineup
    • 4.1 Roster & Lineup
      • 4.1.1 Each team consists of 15 roster spots and will submit a valid lineup each week of the regular and postseason that consists of 9 starting players.
      • 4.1.2 There will not be a limit to the number of players at each position a team can have on their roster.
      • 4.1.3 Limitations will not be placed on the “drop player” status for any players.
    • 4.2 Injured Reserve Slot
      • 4.2.1 Each team will have 1 IR slot separate from their roster that may only be filled by a player designated by the host platform to be IR eligible.
    • 4.3 Submitting Weekly Roster
      • 4.3.1 Individual players on a team roster will be locked in at the start of each player’s game.
      • 4.3.2 If an owner wishes to make a lineup change and is physically unable to do so they may contact the commissioner to make the changes for them.
  • 5 The Draft
    • 5.1 Draft Day & Location
      • 5.1.1 The 2019 draft will be held on Saturday, August 1st
      • 5.1.2 The location of the 2019 draft is at a house on Chickamauga Lake in Decatur, TN
      • 5.1.3 The commissioner will be responsible for purchasing and providing the official draft board and stickers for each season’s draft.
      • 5.1.4 Each team owner is responsible for paying 1/12 of each years draft cost, even if they are not able to attend the draft. By paying 1/12 each league owner is eligible to bring a +1 do the draft without extra cost.
    • 5.2 The Draft & Draft Order
      • 5.2.1 The Saturated Unicorn Draft will be a full auction format starting in 2019. Each team will auction 15 players to fill their roster
      • 5.2.2 The nomination order will be determined by team owners randomly selecting cards from a standard deck of playing cards.
      • 5.2.3 The nomination order will be set the night prior to the draft at a time of the commissioners choosing.
    • 5.3 Auction Budget & Bidding
      • 5.3.1 The auction budget will be $200
      • 5.3.2 All teams must auction 12 players during the auction portion of the draft.
    • 5.4 Overbidding
      • 5.4.1 If a team owner places a bid that exceeds their maximum remaining budget they will be fined $1 FAAB for the first offense and $10 FAAB for every instance thereafter. This fine is levied on the owners in-season FAAB Budget, not against the auction budget.
      • 5.4.2 If an owner overbids on a player that they nominated and bidding completely restarts the player will be re-nominated at $0 and the nominating owner shall then not be allowed bid on this player during that re-bidding round. If none of the eligible owners place a bid on that player, the player in question returns to the undrafted pool and remains auction eligible by all owners, including the owner that committed the overbidding infraction, to be nominated by the standard nomination process outline above.
    • 5.5 Draft Day Requirements
      • 5.5.1 Each team is required to draft a complete roster during the draft.
      • 5.5.2 Failure to abide by these requirements of the draft will result in a $10 FAAB penalty for each position that is not drafted.
      • 5.5.3 Failing to be present at the mandatory TSU draft
